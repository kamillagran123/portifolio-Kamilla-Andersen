Campaign website

Case

Create a support campaign, which consists of a campaign website and associated ads where the goal is to encourage involvement. We chose to shed light on the challenges that electric scooters create for visually impaired people.

Solution

We chose to conduct a user survey to gain a deeper understanding of who the target group actually is and then be able to decide on a concept to be able to reach the target group in the best possible way. We arrived at the following concept: "Not everyone can see the problem". The website contains a video and pictures to shed light on the issue and the challenges that exist, as well as encouragement in the form of text. With this we believe that the target group gets a wake-up call which in turn leads to action.
